Saga Roadmap Summary

From the moment Khan’s wrath threatens millions, The Pantheons launches a cosmic odyssey of tragedy, fear, and awakening that will reshape gods and men alike. Each book deepens the fracture between mortality and divinity:

Book Two – Life’s Not Fair: When grief and death becomes something far darker, the boundaries between justice and vengeance blur, awakening a power that could consume everything.

Book Three – The Power of the Gods: The line between hero and god shatters as time itself bends under obsession, love, and divine consequence.

Book Four – The European King: Power moves into the shadows as old rivalries and buried truths ignite a deadly game of influence, loyalty, and survival.

Book Four – The Congo: New powers emerge across continents as the world discovers its place in a far greater conflict—one that reaches beyond Earth itself. More books after this.
